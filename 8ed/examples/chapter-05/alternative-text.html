Barcelona's Market

The Mercat de la Boquería is the central market that is just off the Rambles. It's an incredible place, full of every kind of food you might happen to need. It took me a long time to get up the nerve to actually take a picture there. You might say I'm kind of a chicken, but since I lived there, it was just sort of strange.
